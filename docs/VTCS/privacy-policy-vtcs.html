Privacy Policy

Voice_To_Content_Studio (VTCS)
Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Voice_To_Content_Studio (VTCS) ("the App") is developed and operated by David McCrory ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

The short version: your content is processed only to provide the App’s functionality. We do not store personal data or build user profiles.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or build profiles using:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Persistent personal profiles or user accounts
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Behavioral tracking data
  • Analytics tied to your identity

Voice and Content Processing

The App allows you to record spoken input and convert it into written and generated content (e.g., posts for X, LinkedIn, newsletters, and summaries).

To provide this functionality:

  • Audio input may be processed temporarily to generate transcription and content
  • Transcription and generated outputs are returned directly to your device
  • We do not store your audio recordings or generated content on our servers

This processing is strictly limited to delivering the App’s core functionality.

Data Storage

Your generated content and transcripts:

  • Are stored locally on your device
  • Remain under your control
  • Can be deleted at any time within the App
  • Are permanently removed when the App is deleted

Data may be included in your iCloud backup according to your Apple device settings, which are governed by Apple’s privacy practices.

Third-Party Processing

The App may rely on third-party services to perform transcription or content generation.

These services:

  • Process data only to fulfill your request
  • Do not receive identifying user profiles from us
  • Are not used for advertising or tracking

We do not integrate:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • User tracking frameworks
  • Behavioral analytics tied to identity

Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device microphone solely to capture spoken input for transcription.

Microphone data is used only when you actively record and is not accessed in the background.

In-App Purchases

VTCS may offer optional in-app purchases handled entirely through Apple’s App Store.

  • We do not receive or store payment information
  • Transactions are managed by Apple
  • We receive only purchase status information

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data, there is no user data retained by us to access, modify, or delete.

You maintain full control of your content directly on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
